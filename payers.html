Network with a specialty infusion provider built for South Carolina.

CoreFlow Specialty Infusion is a South Carolina-licensed specialty infusion pharmacy offering home infusion services as a lower-cost alternative to hospital outpatient and inpatient settings. We are pursuing URAC Specialty Pharmacy v5.0 and ACHC IRX-NO797 accreditation.+

Our clinical and quality framework.

Health system selection

CoreFlow is a preferred home infusion partner for MUSC Health — selected to meet the clinical documentation, nursing quality, and operational standards required by a major SC academic medical center.

Licensure

SC Resident Pharmacy Permit issued by the SC Board of Pharmacy under Reg 99-43(B).

Accreditation

Accredited under URAC Specialty Pharmacy v5.0 and ACHC IRX-NO797 (specialty infusion without sterile compounding). Documentation available to payer credentialing teams upon request.

Nursing model

All infusion services delivered by CoreFlow employed RNs. No contracted or staffed nursing. CRNI-eligible credentialing required as condition of clinical hire.

Cold chain & pharmaceutical handling

Medications sourced as commercially manufactured, pre-compounded products — no sterile compounding. Cold chain meets USP Chapter 659 and 1079. Temperature monitoring documented for every delivery.

Documentation

Clinical documentation returned to the referring prescriber after each infusion visit and made available to payer utilization management for prior authorization and concurrent review.
