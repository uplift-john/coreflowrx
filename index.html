What to expect →

Three steps from referral to infusion.

1

You refer.

Submit online, fax a cover sheet, or call us. We confirm receipt within one business day.

2

We coordinate.

Our team handles insurance verification, prior authorization, medication procurement, and nurse scheduling — and keeps your office informed at every step.

3

Your patient receives care at home.

A CoreFlow RN arrives at the scheduled time with everything needed to administer therapy safely. Clinical notes are returned to your office after each visit.

Value-based infusion care.

We don't measure success by the number of referrals we process. We measure it by what each party actually gets.

Prescribers get

A referral partner who communicates, documents, and doesn't create extra work for your staff.

Patients get

Safe, expert care in their own home — without a hospital waiting room.

Payers get

A lower-cost site of care with the clinical documentation to support it.

Clinical standards that matter.

CoreFlow is dual-accredited through URAC Specialty Pharmacy v5.0 and ACHC IRX-NO797 (specialty infusion without sterile compounding). We hold a South Carolina Board of Pharmacy Resident Pharmacy Permit under Reg 99-43(B).
