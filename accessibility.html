Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last reviewed: January 1, 2026

CoreFlow Specialty Infusion is committed to ensuring that coreflowrx.com is accessible to people with disabilities. We are working to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Our commitment.

We design and build our website so that it can be used by as many people as possible. This includes people who use screen readers, keyboard navigation, or other assistive technologies. We target WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance across all pages and features of coreflowrx.com.

Known limitations.

If you encounter any content on our site that you are unable to access, please let us know. We will work to provide the information in an accessible format as quickly as possible.
