Three things we're built to do better.

In addition to rigorous process and quality control, here's what really makes prescribers and patients choose us again and again.

Easy to refer.

One streamlined referral of your choice: online, fax, or phone. No hoops, no chasing. We confirm we have it within one business day.

Speed to service.

Our goal is to offer your patient an in-home infusion appointment within 24 hours of insurance approval. If they'd rather wait for a day that fits their schedule, that's their call. The speed is there when they want it.

We close the loop.

After every infusion, the nursing note goes back to your office — 100% of the time. Your patient got their infusion, tolerated it well, and here's the follow-up plan. You're never left wondering.

Three steps from referral to infusion.

01

You refer.

Submit online, fax a cover sheet, or call us. We confirm receipt within one business day.

02

We coordinate.

Our team handles insurance verification, prior authorization, medication procurement, and nurse scheduling and keeps your office informed at every step.

03

Your patient receives care at home — fast.

We aim to offer the first appointment within 24 hours of insurance approval. A nurse who works in your patient's community arrives with everything needed to administer therapy safely. After every visit, the nursing note comes back to your office — 100% of the time.

Value-based infusion care.

We don't measure success by the number of referrals we process. We measure it by what each party actually gets.

Prescribers get

A referral partner who communicates, documents, and doesn't create extra work for your staff.

Patients get

Safe, expert care in their own home — without a hospital waiting room.

Payers get

A lower-cost site of care that moves eligible patients out of hospital and infusion-center settings into the home, with the documentation to support it.

Clinical standards that matter.

CoreFlow is pursuing dual accreditation through URAC Specialty Pharmacy v5.0 and ACHC IRX-NO797 (specialty infusion without sterile compounding), anticipated Q4 2026. Accreditation has been initiated and has not yet been awarded. We hold a South Carolina Board of Pharmacy Resident Pharmacy Permit under Reg 99-43(B).
